Live Music

New Orleans is the birthplace of jazz music and there is no shortage of live music anytime of the day or night in any genre. You can always find an open club or street musicians playing on Frenchmen St. Check out some of my favorite venues below or WWOZ 90.7 FM for a listing of live events. Live Wire Music Calender


  • Blue Nile

    Located on Frenchmen St. check out trumpet lengend Kermit Ruffins on Friday nights and local songstess Mykia Jovan Sunday evenings.

    Blue Nile

  • The Maison

    Live music all day on Frenchmen St. Late nights are always a blast with music from local bands and DJs. Check out the inhouse infused libations at the bar.

    The Maison
