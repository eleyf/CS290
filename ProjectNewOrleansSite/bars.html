Cocktail Lounges and Bars

The birthpalce of cocktails has more to offer than just daiquiris. You can find everything from dive bars to chic cocktail lounges. New Orleans is unique in it's 24/7 and open street container liquor laws. That's right, you can find a bar open 24 hours of the day and even get your drink in a "to-go" cup and enjoy it on your way to the next watering hole. Here's a few of my favorite places for a good drink.


  • Loa

    Discretely located downtown in the lobby of the International House Hotel, this lounge offers a wide variety of spirits. The staff is superb in their service and knowledge of mixing drinks. Be sure to order something made with one of their homemade syrups or bitters.

    Loa

  • Cure

    Sophisticated lounge located in the Carrollton neighborhood serving up some of the most unique cocktails in town. With ingredients ranging from apricot, chocolate, and rosemary, there's a cocktail sure to satisfy every part of your palatte.

    Cure
