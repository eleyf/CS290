Restaurants

New Orleans is a city of many cultures blended into one and it's cuisine is the same. Influences from Africa, France, East Asia, and the Caribbean all come together for delicious vegan dining experiences. Here's a few of my favs.


  • Dreamy Weenies

    Offering 4 types of vegan saugae and over 30 types of toppings. Come hungry as the portions are large. Try the unique "New Orleans Style" toppings for there unique combinations.

    Dreamy Weenies

  • Carmo

    Ecclectic international cuisine with plenty of vegan and vegetarian options. The juice bar is the best in town as they serve fresh squeezed tropical fruits that are a rarity in the states such as soursop, caja, passion fruit, and sugar cane.

    Carmo

  • Meals From The Heart Cafe

    Located in the heart of the French Market serving up traditional New Orleans cuisine with a vegan flare and fresh squeezed orange juice. The menu includes vegan adaptations of crab cakes and remoulade, po' boys, tacos, and pancakes,

    Meals From The Heart Cafe

  • Tal's Hummus

    Located Uptown on Magazine St. serving traditional Mediterranean cuisine like hummus, falafel, and couscous, with the main attraction being the vegan chicken shawarma.

    Tal's Hummus
