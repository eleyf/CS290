Streetcars


Getting around New Orleans by streetcar is a great way to see the city. They glide underneath grand live oaks and past stately mansions, while offering up relaxing views of the beauty, charm, and history of the city. There are four different lines: St. Charles, Canal Street, Rampart and the Riverfront, each of which originates downtown but takes you different parts of the city. A one way trip cost $1.25 and an all day pass $3.00, making it the most economical way to get around town. Hop on the St. Charles line, grab a window seat and enjoythe ride.
