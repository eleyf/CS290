Parades

New Orleans Mardi Gras season parades are it's most famous but official and unofficial parades happen year round.


Second Line Parades

Another New Orleans tradition, every Sunday except during Mardi Gras and the sweltering hot months of July and August you can find impromptu street parades lead by brass bands marching through the city. There are plenty of local venders along the route to supply great food and drinks along the way. Find upcoming parades here, join the procession and dance in the streets for a great Sunday afternoon.
